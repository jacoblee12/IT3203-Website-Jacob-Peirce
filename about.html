About Us

Here at J&J, we are fully committed to our animals and their well being. Founded in 2023, we began as a small company with its two founders, Jacob and Jennifer, and we have grown to over 20 people!


We are in the business of giving a forever home to every pet. We also work in animal conservation and with our local partners the Athens Humane Society to provide the best care and sustainable practices to new and future pet owners.


Check out our events page to find out about our community events, and support us by buying our merch!
